What’s this?

Every animator needs a demo reel and, more importantly, somewhere to put it online. Youtube and Vimeo are pretty great, but they’re not the best solution for playing animation.

As an animator you want to flip through keyframes and savor each frame one at a time. Flipbook.js is made specifically to playback animation!

Here are some of the cool things you can do with Flipbook.js:

  • Step through individual frames
  • Set keyframes and skip between them
  • Loop the video

How’s it work?

Flipbook.js has a bunch of keyboard shortcuts set-up to speed up your workflow:

  • Spacebar
    Play/pause video
  • L
    Turn looping on/off
  • Go to next frame
  • Go to previous frame
  • F
    Set a keyframe
  • Skip to next keyframe
  • Skip to previous keyframe
